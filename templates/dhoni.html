Mahendra Singh Dhoni Page

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, born on July 7, 1981, is a legendary Indian cricketer and former captain known for his cool demeanor and exceptional leadership. Renowned as one of the best finishers in limited-overs cricket, he led India to victories in the 2007 T20 World Cup, the 2011 ODI World Cup, and the 2013 Champions Trophy. Dhoni's strategic acumen and calm under pressure have made him an iconic figure in cricket.
